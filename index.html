Privacy Policy – Echo Mart

Last updated: 20 December 2025

Echo Mart (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Echo Mart mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

  • Mobile number (used for login, OTP verification, and order communication)
  • Delivery address (used only to deliver orders)
  • Location data (optional, used to improve delivery accuracy and nearby service availability)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name
  • Email address

How We Use Your Information

  • User authentication using OTP
  • Processing grocery orders and deliveries
  • Providing order updates and customer support
  • Improving app functionality and performance

Third-Party Services

Echo Mart uses trusted third-party services to provide core app functionality. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • OTP / SMS Message Services: Used for secure user authentication and account verification through one-time passwords (OTP). Mobile numbers are used strictly for authentication and security purposes. Echo Mart does not read personal messages or access SMS content other than OTP verification.
  • Firebase: Used for phone number authentication, app performance monitoring, and crash reporting.
  • Payment Gateways: Used to process secure online payments. Echo Mart does not store card or payment details.
  • Map & Location Services: Used to show nearby services and improve delivery accuracy.

Data Sharing

Echo Mart does not sell, rent, or trade user personal data. Information is shared only with trusted service providers when required to deliver app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect user data, including secure communication and restricted access. However, no digital system is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Echo Mart does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your account and associated data. You may contact us for any such requests.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
