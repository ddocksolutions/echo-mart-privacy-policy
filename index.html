Privacy Policy – Echo Mart

Last updated: 20 December 2025

Echo Mart (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Echo Mart mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Mobile number (for login and order communication)
  • Delivery address (for order delivery)
  • Location data (optional, used to improve delivery accuracy)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name
  • Email address

How We Use Your Information

  • Process grocery orders and deliveries
  • User authentication via mobile number
  • Improve app functionality and performance

Third-Party Services

Echo Mart may use trusted third-party services such as Firebase, payment gateways, and map services. These services may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data using secure communication and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Echo Mart does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
