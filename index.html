Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Ddocksolutions ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information through the Echo Mart Android mobile application (package name: com.echomart.store) available on the Google Play Store.

Echo Mart is a local grocery ordering application intended for use in India. By using this application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and applicable laws of India. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Name and mobile number (for OTP-based login)
  • Delivery address and location (for order delivery)
  • Order and transaction details
  • Device and app usage information for security and performance

We do not collect biometric data, facial recognition data, or sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and deliver orders
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

Third-Party Services

The Echo Mart app may use trusted third-party services for app functionality, analytics, and payments, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, analytics, crash reporting)
  • Payment gateway providers for secure online transactions

We do not store or process users’ debit card, credit card, or UPI credentials on our servers. Payment information is handled securely by authorized third-party payment providers.

Information Sharing

We may share information only when necessary to provide our services, such as:

  • With delivery partners to complete orders
  • With payment providers to process transactions
  • When required by law or legal process

We do not sell or rent users’ personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We follow reasonable security practices to protect personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Users may request deletion of their personal data by contacting us at the email address provided below. Account deletion may be delayed if required to resolve disputes, complete transactions, or comply with legal obligations.
